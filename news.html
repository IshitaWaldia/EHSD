About EHSD at NIIT University

The EHSD Research Centre at NIIT University focuses on renewable energy, sustainable technologies, and Internet of Things (IoT). The centre works on innovative solutions to address global energy challenges and environmental sustainability. A major area of research includes herbal batteries, using plant-based materials to develop eco-friendly energy storage systems. Additionally, EHSD is advancing IoT applications for smart grids and energy-efficient buildings, promoting sustainability.

EHSD also provides Capstone Projects, where students design real-world solutions in energy-efficient systems and renewable energy storage, bridging the gap between academia and industry.
