The Research Centre for Energy Harvesting System Design (EHSD) aims to develop interdisciplinary strengths in science and technology issues related to the sustainable development of energy solutions. EHSD research centre provides integrated solutions for obstacles in energy efficiency and renewable energy storage systems. The centre's mission is to design Eco-friendly Herbal batteries as next-gen power sources for electronic gadgets, sensors, and other future devices. We focus on harvesting electrical energy from plants/trees to autonomously power consumer devices.

EHSD's vision is to lead towards a 100% carbon-free future energy system.

Research Areas

Renewable Energy and IoT
Clean and renewable energy, IoT-enabled green energy sources, hybrid renewable energy systems, smart energy generation systems.

Eco-friendly energy storage systems for a safer world, battery prototyping, and performance analysis.

Energy Harvesting Integrated Circuit
Hybrid energy IC for IoT devices, design of EHS for plant energy storage, low-power design for integrated energy systems.
